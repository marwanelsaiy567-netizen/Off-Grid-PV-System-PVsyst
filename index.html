The system tilt and orientation angles were fully optimized in relation to winter incident irradiation profiles to guarantee reliable grid-free performance during lower solar yield intervals.

Engineering Evaluation & Conclusion

The comprehensive simulation results verified that the designed standalone photovoltaic installation successfully meets the targeted residential demand with an excellent annual Solar Fraction of 92.96%. The observed system dropouts and collection constraints remain strictly within the standard thermodynamic parameters of photovoltaic modules and internal charge storage cycle tolerances. Ultimately, the system establishes a highly practical, reliable, and technically viable engineering strategy for autonomous, environment-friendly electricity supply in remote off-grid domains.
